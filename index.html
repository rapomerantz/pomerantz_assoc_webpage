About Us

We help build profitable businesses.

Greg Pomerantz was trained in "blue chip" companies like PepsiCo and ConAgra, and then moved into fast-growth, entrepeneurial environments like NordicTrack and Miracle-Ear. For the last 15+ years Greg has worked with privately owned and emerging businesses.

We work with a broad range of industries to develop sales and marketing strategies, commercialize and launch new products, and establish new distribution channels for small and mid-sized companies. Beyond simply creating a plan, we actively engage in business development, performing sales calls for our clients to validate new products and uncover new customers.

With more than 30 years of experience, from start-ups to Fortune 100 companies, Pomerantz & Associates can help you build an affordable, actionalbe roadmap to increase sales and profitability.

Industry experience:

  • Retail
  • Healthcare

Greg has a B.A from the University of California, Santa Barbara and an M.B.A from the Kellogg School of Management at Northwestern University.
